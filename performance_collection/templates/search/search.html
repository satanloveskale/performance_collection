This collection supports the programs of the Faculty's ensembles.


Borrowing is limited to current students/faculty and members of Choirs Ontario and Orchestras Canada.

The Performance Collection office is open Monday to Friday from 1:00pm to 4:30pm, closed on holidays.

Contact : Karen Wiseman


  • For single copies of choral works, search the UTL catalogue, and the Sheet Music database.
  • Choral music in public domain can also be found at Choral Public Domain Library (CPDL)
  • For individual orchestral parts, consult also The Orchestra Musician's CD-ROM Library
    (M1000 .O65 V. 1-12) at the Music Library Circulation Desk.
  • Another free source for orchestral parts is the International Music Score Library Project , (IMSLP) for parts available in the public domain.
